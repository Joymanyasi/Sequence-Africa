About

Built by a scientist who works at the gap

Genomic data has the power to transform how Africa responds to disease — but only if the right systems, training, and people are in place.

Joy Manyasi Kabaka is a molecular biologist focused on applied genomics, sequencing workflows, and scenario-based genomics education for African clinical and research systems.

NGS Hub exists because most genomics training was not built for African contexts. This one is.
